Our Story

At Get Bready with Genny, we believe that the best bread is made with love, patience, and the finest natural ingredients. Our journey began in a small kitchen, where Genny, the heart and soul of the bakery, began experimenting with sourdough recipes passed down through generations. From the first loaf she baked to the current selection of artisanal breads we offer today, every piece is crafted with care and a deep respect for the art of baking. Over the years, our passion for baking has only grown. What started as a hobby soon became a local favorite, and today, we’re proud to serve our community with a variety of sourdough loaves that are as flavorful as they are wholesome. We use only the best organic ingredients, and our baking process focuses on preserving the unique flavors and textures of each loaf. We’re more than just a bakery – we’re a family. Each loaf we bake is a piece of our story, and we’re thrilled to share it with you. Whether you’re here for the classic sourdough, or you’re looking to try something new, we invite you to experience the warmth, heart, and flavor that makes Get Bready with Genny so special.
